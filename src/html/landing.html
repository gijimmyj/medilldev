Integrated Marketing Communications

The Integrated Marketing Communications Degree (IMC) was founded at Medill.

IMC started at Medill, and here, you’ll learn to create innovative marketing communications strategies and solve business problems by working collaboratively with both global companies and your peers. Our three graduate programs and one undergraduate certificate will teach you the IMC “way of thinking” that balances qualitative and quantitative components. Medill IMC will help set you apart from others in the industry.
